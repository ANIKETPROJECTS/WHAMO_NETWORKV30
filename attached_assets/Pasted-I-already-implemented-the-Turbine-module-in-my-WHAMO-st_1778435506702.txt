OBJECTIVE

UNDERSTAND THE DATA FORMAT

The uploaded file structure is:

SPECIFIC SPEED (US/SI): 30 / 115
NUMGATES: 9

GATE: 20.00
HEAD Q TORQUE

GATE: 30.00
HEAD Q TORQUE
...

Each block contains:

Column	Meaning
HEAD	Normalized head
Q	Flow
TORQUE	Torque coefficient / power coefficient

The library replaces manually typed TCHAR matrices with real hill-chart-based turbine curves.
